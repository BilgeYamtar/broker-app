## Turkce

### Giris

Bu gizlilik politikasi, BilgeYamtar tarafindan gelistirilen **Yuk Portfoyu** mobil uygulamasinin kullanici verilerini nasil isledigini aciklamaktadir.

### Veri Toplama

Yuk Portfoyu **hicbir kisisel veri toplamaz.** Uygulama tamamen cevrimdisi calisir ve kullanici verilerini herhangi bir sunucuya iletmez.

### Yerel Veri Depolama

Uygulama icinde girilen tum bilgiler (gemi profilleri, yuk kayitlari, belgeler, charter party sozlesmeleri, fizibilite raporlari ve diger veriler) yalnizca cihazinizda yerel olarak saklanir. Bu veriler:

- Cihazinizdaki SQLite veritabaninda tutulur
- Hicbir dis sunucuya gonderilmez
- Hicbir ucuncu tarafla paylasilmaz
- Uygulamayi sildiginizde kalici olarak silinir

### Kamera ve Fotograflar

Uygulama, broker notlarina ve gemi profillerine fotograf eklemek amaciyla cihaz kamerasina ve fotograf galerisine erisim izni talep edebilir. Cekilen veya secilen fotograflar:

- Yalnizca uygulamanin yerel depolama alaninda (sandbox) saklanir
- Hicbir sunucuya yuklenmez
- Hicbir ucuncu tarafla paylasilmaz

### Analitik ve Izleme

Yuk Portfoyu:

- Analitik araci kullanmaz
- Kullanici davranislarini izlemez
- Reklam gostermez
- Reklam izleme teknolojileri icermez
- Konum verisi toplamaz

### Ucuncu Taraf Hizmetleri

Uygulama, abonelik yonetimi icin Apple App Store ve Google Play Store odeme altyapilarini kullanabilir. Bu islemler tamamen Apple ve Google'in kendi gizlilik politikalari kapsaminda yurutulur. Gelistirici olarak bu islemlere iliskin odeme bilgilerine erismemiz mumkun degildir.

### Cocuklarin Gizliligi

Uygulama cocuklardan bilerek veri toplamaz. Uygulama icerigi profesyonel denizcilik sektorune yoneliktir.

### Degisiklikler

Bu gizlilik politikasi zaman zaman guncellenebilir. Degisiklikler bu sayfada yayinlanir ve "Son guncelleme" tarihi revize edilir.

## English

### Introduction

This privacy policy describes how the **Yuk Portfoyu** mobile application, developed by BilgeYamtar, handles user data.

### Data Collection

Yuk Portfoyu **does not collect any personal data.** The application operates entirely offline and does not transmit user data to any server.

### Local Data Storage

All information entered within the application (vessel profiles, cargo records, documents, charter party agreements, feasibility reports, and other data) is stored locally on your device only. This data:

- Is kept in a SQLite database on your device
- Is never sent to any external server
- Is never shared with any third party
- Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the application

### Camera and Photos

The application may request access to your device camera and photo library for the purpose of attaching photos to broker notes and vessel profiles. Photos captured or selected:

- Are stored only in the application's local storage sandbox
- Are never uploaded to any server
- Are never shared with any third party

### Analytics and Tracking

Yuk Portfoyu:

- Does not use any analytics tools
- Does not track user behavior
- Does not display advertisements
- Does not contain advertising tracking technologies
- Does not collect location data

### Third-Party Services

The application may use Apple App Store and Google Play Store payment infrastructure for subscription management. These transactions are conducted entirely under the respective privacy policies of Apple and Google. As the developer, we do not have access to payment information related to these transactions.

### Children's Privacy

The application does not knowingly collect data from children. The application content is intended for the professional maritime industry.

### Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be published on this page and the "Last updated" date will be revised.

## English Description (App Store)

Yuk Portfoyu is a professional mobile platform designed for ship brokers and maritime operations. It combines cargo-vessel compliance analysis, route planning, cost estimation, and maritime document management into a single app to streamline your brokerage workflow.

KEY FEATURES

- Cargo Portfolio: Record cargo details including type, weight, volume, hazard class, temperature control, and special handling requirements.

- Vessel Profiles: Fleet management with vessel specifications, photos, and broker notes in one place.

- Compliance Analysis: AI-powered feasibility reports analyzing cargo-vessel compatibility across hull integrity, thermal management, and ECA compliance dimensions.

- Route Planning: Calculate distances between 8,000+ ports, fuel consumption, and voyage costs.

- Pre-Voyage Estimation: Comprehensive voyage cost projections covering fuel, charter, port costs, freight revenue, and insurance expenses.

- Freight Calculator: Detailed freight and commission calculations for Voyage Charter, Time Charter, and Bareboat Charter types.

- Stowage & Volume Calculator: Stowage Factor analysis, reference table, and hold capacity calculations.

- Charter Party (C/P): Prepare charter agreements with GENCON, ASBATANKVOY, NYPE, BALTIME, and SHELLVOY templates. Built-in demurrage/despatch calculator.

- Maritime Documents: NOR, SOF, LOR, B/L, Mate's Receipt, Cargo Manifest, and port checklists. Auto-fill from vessel/cargo profiles.

- IMDG Dangerous Goods Guide: Stowage rules, fire response, spill procedures, PPE, and placard information for all 9 classes.

- PDF & Text Sharing: Share reports and documents as PDF or plain text.

OFFLINE CAPABILITY
All data is stored locally on your device. Continue working without an internet connection.

PREMIUM
The free plan includes 3 vessels, 5 cargoes, and 3 feasibility analyses per day. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited access, PDF export, all documents, charter party, pre-voyage, freight, and stowage calculators.
